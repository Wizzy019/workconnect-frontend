WorkConnect Style Guide

(Clean, Fresh, Growth-Focused Theme)

---

1. Color Palette

Primary Colors

Emerald Green (Main): #059669

Emerald Dark (Hover): #047857

Emerald Light: #10B981

Neutrals

Neutral Dark: #374151

Neutral Medium: #6B7280

Neutral Light: #E5E7EB

Background: #F0FDF4

Pure White: #FFFFFF

Accent

Lime Accent: #84CC16 (highlights, CTAs, badges)

---

2. Typography

Font Family

Roboto for headings and body.

Professional, easy to read, great for dashboards and listings.

Font Sizes (Tailwind)

H1: text-4xl font-bold

H2: text-3xl font-semibold

H3: text-2xl font-semibold

Body Large: text-lg

Body: text-base

Caption: text-sm text-neutral-medium

---

3. Spacing System

XS: p-2

SM: p-4

MD: p-6

LG: p-8

XL: p-10

Components should feel airy, breathable, and consistent.

---

4. Buttons

Primary Button

Solid Green: bg-emerald-600 text-white px-6 py-3 rounded-lg font-medium hover:bg-emerald-700 transition

Secondary Button

Outline: border border-emerald-600 text-emerald-600 px-6 py-3 rounded-lg hover:bg-emerald-50

Accent Button

Lime: bg-lime-500 text-white px-6 py-3 rounded-md hover:bg-lime-600

---

5. Card / Container Styles

bg-white rounded-xl shadow-md p-6 border border-neutral-light

Hover variant: hover:shadow-lg hover:-translate-y-1 transition

---

6. Shadows

Soft: shadow-md

Strong: shadow-lg

Smooth + modern: shadow-[0_4px_20px_rgba(0,0,0,0.05)]

---

7. Border Radius

General: rounded-lg

Buttons: rounded-md

Cards: rounded-xl

Modals: rounded-2xl

---

8. Usage Rules

60 / 30 / 10 Color Rule: 60% Neutral/background, 30% Primary green, 10% Lime accents

Typography Rule: Roboto for all text, headings always heavier than body
